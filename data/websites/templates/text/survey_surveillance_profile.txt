## Template Guidance

**Primary goal:** Provide basic information about a survey, collection of
surveys, or surveillance system and how the results impact public health.

Can also include:

* Purpose
  * Types of surveys or systems used
  * What’s collected and how it’s collected
  * How the data is used
  * Key findings
  * Links to data
  * Information around programs and participants
  * Resources
